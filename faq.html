About Orchid Tree

Orchid Tree is a boutique nature estate and private retreat about forty-five minutes from Whitefield, Bengaluru. Eleven rooms sit within a living forest, designed for rest rather than crowds — no buffets, no itinerary, and nowhere you need to be.

It is a boutique nature estate — somewhere between the two. You get full hospitality (breakfast, a restaurant, pool, steam room and activities) but the calm and privacy of a home, with only eleven rooms and no resort crowds.

Orchid Tree was created by Pradeep Kuppa Swamy, the landscape architect behind Infosys Mysore, Biocon and Britannia, and a winner of the World Architecture Award. The cottages were built around living trees rather than clearing them.

Yes. The estate runs on solar power and recycled water, uses eco-friendly toiletries, and was built around the existing trees so the forest stays standing.

Rooms & stays

Eleven rooms, across four types, designed for calm rather than crowds.

Four: Couple Rooms by the Pool, Family Rooms by the Pool, Couple Garden Cottages, and the Family Garden Cottage (Chandana). Each room is named after a tree on the estate.

The poolside rooms are bright and overlook the water, clustered near the pool. The garden cottages are built around living trees, set a little apart among the forest, with open-to-sky baths and private steam rooms.

A single Couple Room sleeps two adults plus a child; Family Rooms sleep up to four; and Chandana, the family cottage, sleeps up to six. Across all eleven rooms the estate comfortably hosts a large group — ideal for full-property buyouts.

Chandana, the Family Garden Cottage, sleeps up to six and is the largest and only one of its kind on the estate, with a private patio, a private steam room and a bath open to the sky.

Yes, every room is fully air conditioned.

Check-in is from 2 PM and check-out is by 11 AM. Please carry a government photo ID for check-in.
