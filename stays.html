With every stay

The good things,
already yours

Breakfast, included

Cooked fresh each morning and plated, never a buffet. Lunch and dinner are pre-ordered from the restaurant.

Pool, steam and the run of it

The ozone pool, the common steam room, the open-air gym, billiards and games, all part of the stay.

The whole estate

Forest trails, the farm and buggy, the bonfire and a sky full of stars. Activities across the grounds, on the house.

Thoughtful rooms

Air conditioned, sun-lit baths, copper water jugs, millet snacks, a kettle and fresh linen. Pets welcome in most.

Proudly BYOB

Bring your own bottle and pour it slowly in the outdoor lounge, under the stars when the bonfire catches.

No clock, no crowd

An estate kept small on purpose. No itinerary, no day visitors, no rush. The day is yours to waste beautifully.
