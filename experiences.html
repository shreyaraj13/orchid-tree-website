The shape of a day

How a day unfolds at our nature estate near Bangalore

Three parts to it, none of them required. Wake when you wake. Wander when you feel like wandering. The estate keeps its own slow time, and you are welcome to keep yours. What follows is one day, hour by hour.

06:30Morning

Mornings you don't set an alarm for

Birdsong first, then a breakfast you didn't have to plan. The pool is warm and empty before the day finds it. You will hear the forest waking before you see a single other guest.

15:00Afternoon

Afternoons that go nowhere

Take the buggy down to the farm and pull a tomato off the vine. Lose an hour to carrom and a paperback. Steam, then sleep. The afternoon will not ask anything of you.

19:30Evening

Evenings around a fire

As the light drops, there is a bonfire, a sky thick with stars, and your own bottle poured slowly. Karaoke, if the mood turns. On a clear night you will count more stars than you have in years.
